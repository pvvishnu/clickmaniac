About

I work in IT by profession, where structure, systems, and problem-solving shape most of my day. Photography is the space in between. It is the hobby that lets me step away from screens, notice light more carefully, and capture places, moods, and fleeting details that might otherwise pass unnoticed.

I usually shoot whenever time opens up: a short drive, a quiet evening, a sudden patch of good weather, or a moment that simply feels worth keeping. My images are less about perfection and more about presence. They reflect how I see the world when I finally pause long enough to really look.
